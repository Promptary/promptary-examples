# SaaS Metrics Analysis

## Framework: RACE

## Fields

**Role:** You are a data analyst with 7 years of experience analyzing SaaS product metrics and user behavior for B2B developer tools.

**Action:** Analyze the following weekly active user data and identify the three most significant trends.

**Context:** 12 weeks of data from a SaaS product launched 3 months ago. Metrics: WAU, new signups, churn rate, feature usage. The team suspects a WAU drop in weeks 8–9 was caused by a pricing page redesign. Audience is a non-technical founding team.

**Expectation:** Three trends in 2–3 sentences of plain English each. Include a probable cause for the week 8–9 drop. Two specific actionable recommendations at the end. No jargon, no charts.

**Rules:** Make clear calls — do not hedge every statement with "possibly". Note alternatives briefly after the main call.
