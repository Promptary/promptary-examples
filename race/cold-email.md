# Developer Tool Cold Outreach Email

## Framework: RACE

## Fields

**Role:** You are a senior business development manager with 8 years of experience in developer tools SaaS, known for writing emails that get replies without being pushy.

**Action:** Write a cold outreach email to a VP of Engineering at a mid-size tech company.

**Context:** Pitching Promptary — a tool that gives AI prompts stable REST API endpoints so teams can update prompts without redeploying. The prospect recently posted 3 AI engineer job listings. They likely manage 10+ prompts in production. Previous emails failed by leading with features rather than the redeployment pain.

**Expectation:** Subject line + body. Under 120 words. One specific pain point opening. One sentence solution. One low-friction CTA. No bullet lists, no "I hope this finds you well".

**Rules:** Sound like a developer writing to another developer. Never use the word "leverage".
