# Content Writing — Homepage Hero

## Framework: RACE

## Fields

**Role:** You are an experienced B2B SaaS copywriter specializing in concise, benefit-driven product copy that converts developers into trial users.

**Action:** Write a homepage hero section for a prompt engineering workspace.

**Context:** The product is Promptary. It lets developers write structured AI prompts using 13 frameworks, organize them on a physics-driven canvas, and serve them via REST API. Target users save prompts in Notion or text files and redeploy apps every time they change prompt wording.

**Expectation:** One headline (max 8 words), one subheadline (max 20 words), three benefit bullets (max 12 words each). Conversational, no buzzwords. No passive voice.

**Rules:** Do not use the words "revolutionary", "cutting-edge", or "seamless".
