# Homepage Signup CTA

## Framework: TAG

## Fields

**Task:** Homepage call-to-action button label and supporting subtext for a free developer tool with a paid API tier.

**Action:** Write the button label and one sentence of supporting subtext that appears directly below the CTA button.

**Goal:** Get a visiting developer to click and sign up without hesitation. Remove the two most common hesitation points: "Do I need a credit card?" and "What am I committing to?" Make zero commitment feel obvious.
