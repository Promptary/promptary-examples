# Technical Interview Question

## Framework: TAG

## Fields

**Task:** A senior backend engineer role at a company building AI-powered products. The candidate claims expertise in prompt engineering and API design.

**Action:** Write 3 technical interview questions that assess whether the candidate actually has hands-on experience with prompt management in production, not just theoretical knowledge.

**Goal:** Questions that experienced practitioners answer differently from people who've only read about it. The questions should reveal real production experience, not just familiarity with the terminology.
