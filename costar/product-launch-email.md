# Product Launch Email — Developer Audience

## Framework: CO-STAR

## Fields

**Context:** Promptary has just launched its Developer plan which gives developers a stable REST API endpoint for every saved AI prompt. Recipients are on the free waitlist and have not yet signed up.

**Objective:** Get the reader to sign up for the Developer plan within 48 hours by showing them the exact problem it solves — updating prompts without redeploying.

**Style:** Direct and technical — like a developer writing to another developer, not a marketer writing to a lead. No hype.

**Tone:** Excited but grounded. Peer-to-peer. The enthusiasm of a builder who just shipped something they're proud of.

**Audience:** Indie developers and small startup teams who already build AI-powered apps and understand REST APIs. They are skeptical of SaaS tools that overpromise.

**Response:** Email with subject line. Under 180 words. No bullet lists. One CTA link at the end.
