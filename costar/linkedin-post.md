# LinkedIn Founder Post — Product Launch

## Framework: CO-STAR

## Fields

**Context:** I just launched Promptary, a tool that gives every AI prompt a stable REST endpoint. I built it in a few weeks after getting frustrated with hardcoding prompts and redeploying apps for every wording change.

**Objective:** Build awareness of the prompt management problem and position Promptary as the solution, driving profile visits and free signups.

**Style:** Storytelling — start with the specific frustration I experienced, then the moment I decided to fix it, then what I built.

**Tone:** Authentic founder voice. Honest about the fact it's a small tool solving a specific problem. Not corporate, not salesy, not overly polished.

**Audience:** Developers and technical founders on LinkedIn who are building AI-powered products and will recognize the pain of managing prompts in code.

**Response:** LinkedIn post, 180–220 words. Strong hook in the first line (no "I'm excited to announce"). No hashtag stuffing — max 4 relevant hashtags at the end. End with a genuine question that invites comments.
