# Customer Support Response — API Issue

## Framework: CO-STAR

## Fields

**Context:** A Promptary Developer plan subscriber cannot get their API key to work. They've been trying for 2 hours and are frustrated. The most common cause is forgetting the "Bearer " prefix in the Authorization header.

**Objective:** Resolve the customer's frustration, provide the fix, and leave them feeling supported rather than dismissed — without making them feel stupid for the mistake.

**Style:** Conversational and direct. One paragraph acknowledging the frustration, one paragraph with the fix and why it's a common issue, one sentence CTA.

**Tone:** Warm and technically confident. Like a knowledgeable colleague helping, not a support ticket system responding. Empathetic without being performative.

**Audience:** A developer who is technically capable but has hit a confusing auth issue. They don't need hand-holding — they need the fix and to feel heard.

**Response:** Under 100 words. No bullet lists. No "Dear [Name]". No "Please don't hesitate to reach out". Include the correct Authorization header format in inline code.
