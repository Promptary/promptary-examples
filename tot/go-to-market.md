# Go-to-Market Channel Strategy

## Framework: Tree-of-Thought

## Fields

**Problem:** Promptary needs to choose its primary customer acquisition channel for the next 6 months. Constraints: solo founder, €500/month marketing budget, technical developer audience. Options: content marketing (SEO/blog), community-driven (Reddit/Discord/HN), or paid acquisition (Google Ads targeting developer keywords).

**Number of branches:** Explore all 3 acquisition channel options.

**Evaluation criteria:** Evaluate each on: (1) time to first paying customer, (2) cost per acquisition, (3) scalability beyond 6 months, (4) fit with a solo founder's available time.

**Selection:** Score 1–5 on each criterion. Recommend the highest scorer. If two options tie, recommend a hybrid and explain how to combine them with a €500 budget.

**Rules:** Be specific about realistic timelines and costs — no vague "it depends". Each branch must include at least one concrete first action the founder could take this week.
