# Financial Break-Even Analysis

## Framework: Chain-of-Thought

## Fields

**Role:** You are a senior financial analyst with expertise in SaaS unit economics and break-even modeling.

**Task:** Calculate whether our SaaS business will reach profitability by Q4 2026 given the following figures: MRR $18,000, monthly churn 3.2%, new customer acquisition 45/month, average deal size $400/month, burn rate $42,000/month, runway 8 months.

**Think step by step:** Show your reasoning at each stage: first calculate net new MRR per month, then project cumulative MRR month by month, then identify the month where MRR covers the burn rate. Show each calculation explicitly before moving to the next.

**Output format:** A month-by-month projection table (Month, MRR, Gap to breakeven), followed by a clear Yes/No verdict on Q4 profitability, then a one-paragraph summary of the two most important drivers.

**Rules:** Use only the numbers provided. State any assumptions explicitly. Do not use external data or industry benchmarks.
