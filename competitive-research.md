# SEO Content Strategy Plan

## Framework: Plan-and-Solve

## Fields

**Task:** Create a 90-day SEO content strategy for Promptary targeting developers searching for prompt engineering tools, frameworks, and alternatives to shuttered competitors.

**Plan instructions:** First, outline the strategy framework: keyword categories to target, content types to produce, publishing cadence, and success metrics. Do not write any content yet — plan only.

**Solve instructions:** Execute the plan: for each keyword category, produce 3 specific article titles with target keyword, estimated competition level (low/medium/high), and a 2-sentence brief describing what the article should cover.

**Output format:** Section 1: Strategy framework (bulleted). Section 2: Content calendar organized by keyword category, with titles, target keywords, competition level, and brief. Section 3: 30/60/90-day milestones for measuring success.

**Rules:** Every article title must include the target keyword naturally. No generic titles like "The Ultimate Guide to...". Competition levels must be justified in one phrase. All keywords must be ones developers actually search.
