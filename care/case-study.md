# Customer Case Study

## Framework: CARE

## Fields

**Context:** A Promptary Developer plan user is an indie developer who built an AI-powered writing assistant. Before Promptary, they were hardcoding system prompts in their app and redeploying 2–3 times per week just to tweak prompt wording. After switching to Promptary, they update prompts in real time and their deploy frequency dropped to once per week.

**Action:** Write a short case study for the Promptary website featuring this developer's story.

**Result:** 200-word case study with: a pull quote, the before/after situation, the specific metric (deploy frequency), and a one-sentence conclusion. Format as a website testimonial block, not a press release.

**Example:** Vercel case studies — lead with the outcome metric, then the story, then a direct quote from the customer. No corporate language. Reads like a real person talking about a real problem.
