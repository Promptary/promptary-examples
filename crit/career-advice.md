# Career Transition Advice

## Framework: CRIT

## Fields

**Context:** I am a software engineer with 6 years of backend experience considering a transition into AI engineering or prompt engineering as a specialization. I want to understand whether this is a smart career move and how to position myself.

**Role:** You are an executive career coach who has helped 200+ software engineers navigate AI-related career transitions, with current knowledge of the 2026 AI job market.

**Interview:** Before giving advice, ask me 4 questions to understand: my current tech stack and experience level, what specifically attracts me to AI engineering, whether I prefer individual contributor or lead roles, and my timeline and risk tolerance.

**Task:** After the interview, provide: an honest assessment of the transition's difficulty and likelihood of success given my background, a 90-day skill development plan, the 3 most important things to add to my portfolio, and the job titles to target first.

## Assembled Prompt
