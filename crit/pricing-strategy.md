# Pricing Strategy Review

## Framework: CRIT

## Fields

**Context:** Promptary currently has three plans: Personal (free), Developer (€9/month), and Team (€125/month for 5 seats). I'm not sure if the price points are right or whether the feature gating between plans is optimal for conversion.

**Role:** You are a SaaS pricing strategist with experience optimizing pricing for developer tools with freemium models, having consulted on pricing for 30+ B2B SaaS companies.

**Interview:** Before giving recommendations, ask me 4 questions to understand: current conversion rate from free to paid, average revenue per user, the most common reason users give for not upgrading, and which features free users engage with most.

**Task:** After the interview, provide a pricing analysis with: assessment of current price points vs market benchmarks, 2 alternative pricing structures worth testing, recommended feature gating changes, and the single highest-leverage change to make first.
