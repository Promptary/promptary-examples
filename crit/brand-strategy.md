# Brand Strategy — Developer Tool

## Framework: CRIT

## Fields

**Context:** I am launching Promptary, a prompt engineering workspace for developers and teams. It gives every saved AI prompt a stable REST API endpoint. I need to define brand positioning and core messaging before writing copy or running ads.

**Role:** You are a brand strategist with 15 years of experience positioning B2B developer tools, having worked with companies from seed stage to Series B.

**Interview:** Before writing the strategy, ask me up to 5 targeted questions to understand: my target customer's primary pain, the alternatives they're currently using, my key differentiator, any brand constraints or preferences, and the one thing I want developers to remember about Promptary after one interaction.

**Task:** After the interview, write a one-page brand positioning document with: a positioning statement, 3 core messages (one sentence each), tone of voice guidelines with 3 adjectives and 3 anti-adjectives, and a tagline option.
