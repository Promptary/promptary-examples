# Product Demo Clip — Canvas Animation

## Framework: Video Gen

## Fields

**Scene:** The Promptary physics canvas at night — dark background with 5 glowing topic bubbles (teal, purple, orange, green, pink) each containing 3-4 smaller prompt bubbles orbiting them slowly. The bubbles float and drift gently.

**Motion:** Bubbles pulse slightly as if breathing. Prompt bubbles orbit their topic bubble slowly. One topic bubble grows slightly larger as if accumulating more prompts. A new prompt bubble appears and gravitates toward a topic bubble.

**Camera:** Slow push in from a wide establishing shot to a medium close-up over 8 seconds. Very subtle rotation (3 degrees) adds depth without being distracting.

**Lighting:** Deep dark background (#09090F), each bubble rimlit with its own color, teal accent glow predominates, subtle lens bokeh in the background, soft purple ambient.

**Style:** Cinematic 4K, premium dark UI aesthetic. Similar to Vercel or Linear product launch videos. Not a screen recording — a stylized render.

**Duration:** 8 seconds

**Negative:** Flickering, artifacts, abrupt cuts, text overlays, watermark, faces, people, sudden movements, jitter.

**Parameters:** 4K, 24fps, motion_strength: 0.5, cfg_scale: 7
