# Job Description — AI Engineer

## Framework: APE

## Fields

**Action:** Write a job description for a senior AI engineer role at a B2B SaaS startup.

**Purpose:** Attract experienced AI engineers who want to work on real production systems, not research. The company has already shipped an AI-powered product and needs someone to scale it, not prototype. Generic job descriptions attract generic applicants — this one needs to filter for people who have shipped AI features to real users.

**Expectation:** Job title, 2-sentence company description, responsibilities (5 bullets), requirements (5 bullets), what makes this role different from big tech (1 paragraph). Under 400 words. No "We are looking for a rockstar" or similar. No "competitive salary" without a range.
