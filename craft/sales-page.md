# Pricing Page Sales Copy

## Framework: CRAFT

## Fields

**Context:** The Promptary pricing page needs copy that converts developers who've been on the free plan for 2+ weeks and understand the product but haven't upgraded. The barrier isn't understanding — it's not yet feeling the €9/month is worth it.

**Role:** You are a conversion copywriter specializing in developer tools who understands that developers are allergic to hype and respond to specificity.

**Action:** Write the section of the pricing page directly above the three pricing cards — a 3–4 sentence block that pushes undecided free users to upgrade.

**Format:** 3–4 sentences, no bullet points, no headers. Goes directly above the plan cards. Should feel like a natural bridge from "I like this free tool" to "I should pay for API access".

**Tone:** Direct and specific. Speaks to a real scenario the developer has already experienced: changing a prompt and having to redeploy. No generic "unlock the full potential" language.

**Rules:** Do not use "supercharge", "unlock", "powerful", or "game-changer". One specific number or metric if possible.
