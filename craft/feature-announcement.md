# In-App Feature Announcement

## Framework: CRAFT

## Fields

**Context:** Promptary just launched prompt version history for Team plan users. Every edit to a workspace prompt is automatically saved as a version. Users can view the full history, restore any previous version, and delete old versions they no longer need.

**Role:** You are the founder of Promptary writing a product update notification to existing Team plan users.

**Action:** Write an in-app notification announcing the new version history feature.

**Format:** Under 70 words. Three sentences: what it is, why it matters, how to access it. No marketing language. No exclamation marks.

**Tone:** Founder voice — direct, slightly excited, no fluff. Like a builder announcing something they're genuinely proud of shipping. Not a press release, not a marketing email.

**Rules:** Do not start with "We're excited to announce". Do not use the word "powerful" or "robust".
