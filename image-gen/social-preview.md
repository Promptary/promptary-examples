# Open Graph Social Preview Card

## Framework: Image Gen

## Fields

**Subject:** A clean, dark social preview card for Promptary showing the product name in teal Space Grotesk font, a single glowing bubble node, and a one-line tagline. Minimal. Premium. The kind of image that looks good when a URL is pasted into Slack or Twitter.

**Style:** Flat design, dark background, minimal UI card aesthetic. Reminiscent of Vercel, Railway, or Linear link previews. Not illustrative — graphic design.

**Lighting:** Dark background with subtle radial gradient from center, teal glow from the logo dot element, no dramatic lighting.

**Composition:** Centered composition, 2:1 aspect ratio (1200x630px standard OG size). Logo top-left, main content centered, subtle border or card edge.

**Mood:** Premium, trustworthy, technical. The kind of tool serious developers use. Understated — lets the product speak.

**Negative:** Stock photo elements, people, gradients that look cheap, busy backgrounds, multiple competing focal points, watermark.

**Parameters:** --ar 1200:630 --style raw --v 6.1 --q 2
