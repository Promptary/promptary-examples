# Competitive Landscape Analysis

## Framework: Plan-and-Solve

## Fields

**Task:** Analyze the prompt management tools market and produce a competitive positioning map for Promptary, a prompt engineering workspace with a REST API endpoint per prompt.

**Plan instructions:** First, devise a step-by-step research plan. Identify: what categories of tools to examine, what dimensions to compare them on, and what data points are needed for each. List the plan steps before doing any analysis.

**Solve instructions:** Then carry out each step of the plan in order. For each competitor, gather: primary audience, key differentiator, pricing model, and the one thing Promptary does that they do not. Show your work at each step.

**Output format:** Step 1: the research plan (numbered list). Step 2: competitor profiles (one per tool, 4 bullet points each). Step 3: a positioning matrix table. Step 4: a 3-sentence Promptary positioning statement derived from the gaps found.

**Rules:** Minimum 5 competitors. Do not include tools that are discontinued or have no active users in 2026. The positioning statement must be derived from the analysis — not invented beforehand.
