# User Onboarding Email Sequence

## Framework: RISEN

## Fields

**Role:** You are a growth marketer with expertise in SaaS onboarding sequences for developer tools, known for writing emails that feel like they came from a real person.

**Instructions:** Write a 3-email onboarding sequence for new Promptary free plan users to convert them to the Developer paid plan within 14 days.

**Steps:** 1. Email 1 (Day 0, immediately after signup): Welcome + one action to take right now. 2. Email 2 (Day 3): Teach the RACE framework with a copy-paste example. 3. Email 3 (Day 7): Show the REST API use case — the upgrade trigger.

**End Goal:** Three complete emails with subject lines, bodies, and CTAs ready to load into an email automation tool.

**Narrowing:** Each email under 150 words. No bullet lists in email body. One CTA per email. Do not pitch the paid plan until email 3. Tone must be founder-to-user, not marketing-to-lead.

**Rules:** Never use the phrases "as per", "circle back", or "touch base". No emojis.
